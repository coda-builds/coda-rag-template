# New Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Acme Corp! This guide walks you through everything you need to do in your first two weeks.

## Before Your First Day

Your line manager will send you a welcome email with:
- Your company email address and temporary password
- A link to complete your Right to Work verification via Veremark
- An invitation to our onboarding Slack channel (#new-joiners)

Please complete the Right to Work verification before Day 1, as IT cannot activate your accounts without it.

## Day 1 – Arrival and Setup

**Morning (9:00–12:00):**
- Collect your access badge from reception (bring photo ID)
- Meet your manager and buddy for a welcome coffee
- IT will set up your laptop – this takes approximately 90 minutes
- Complete the data protection and IT security e-learning modules (links in your welcome email)

**Afternoon (13:00–17:00):**
- Office tour with your buddy
- Set up Slack, Google Workspace, and 1Password
- Book your mandatory HR induction session (see calendar invite)
- Review the Employee Handbook

## Week 1 Checklist

- [ ] Complete all mandatory e-learning (Data Protection, IT Security, Health & Safety)
- [ ] Set up payroll details in the HR portal (hr.acmecorp.com → My Details → Bank Account)
- [ ] Order business cards via the Marketing Portal (if applicable to your role)
- [ ] Join your team's Slack channels and project boards
- [ ] Meet with your manager to agree 30/60/90-day goals
- [ ] Attend the all-hands meeting (held every other Monday at 10:00 am)
- [ ] Set up your email signature using the template in the Brand Portal

## Week 2 Checklist

- [ ] Shadow at least two colleagues from other departments
- [ ] Attend one customer call or product demo (arranged by your manager)
- [ ] Complete the product knowledge quiz in the Learning Hub
- [ ] Book your first 1:1 with your manager (if not already scheduled)
- [ ] Enrol in benefits (health insurance, pension) – deadline is Day 14

## Probation

Your employment includes a 3-month probation period. Your manager will conduct a formal probation review at the end of month 3. If all goes well, you'll receive written confirmation that probation has been passed. If there are any concerns, your manager will raise them with you before the review.

Questions? Reach out to your buddy, manager, or People & Culture team at any time.
