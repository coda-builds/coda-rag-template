# Finance & Expense Procedures

**Owner:** Finance Team | **Version:** 3.1 | **Effective date:** 1 February 2025

## Expense Claims

Employees may claim reimbursement for reasonable business expenses incurred in the performance of their duties. All claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after 30 days will be declined unless accompanied by a written explanation approved by the Finance Director.

### Approved Expense Categories

| Category | Limit (no pre-approval) | Notes |
|----------|------------------------|-------|
| Travel (rail/bus) | £200 per trip | Book via Trainline Business where possible |
| Taxi / rideshare | £50 per journey | Only when public transport unavailable or after 10 pm |
| Flights | £600 economy | Business class requires CFO approval |
| Hotel accommodation | £180 per night (London), £120 elsewhere | Book via TravelPerk |
| Client meals | £75 per person | Itemised receipt required |
| Team meals | £40 per person | Manager approval required |
| Home office equipment | £250 per item | IT must approve before purchase |
| Software subscriptions | £50/month | IT and Finance must approve before subscribing |

### Expenses Requiring Pre-Approval

- Any single expense over £500
- International travel (outside the UK)
- Conference registration fees
- Gifts to clients or partners

Pre-approval requests must be submitted via the Finance Portal with a business justification at least 5 working days before the expense is incurred.

## Expense Submission Process

1. Collect receipts for all expenses (digital or physical).
2. Log in to the Finance Portal (finance.acmecorp.com).
3. Create a new expense report, selecting the appropriate cost centre and project code.
4. Upload receipt images (JPEG or PDF, max 10 MB each).
5. Submit for manager approval.
6. Once approved, reimbursement is processed with the next payroll run (25th of each month) if submitted by the 15th. Claims submitted after the 15th are processed the following month.

## Purchase Orders

Any purchase committing the company to recurring expenditure or exceeding £1,000 in total value requires a Purchase Order (PO). To raise a PO:

1. Submit a PO request in the Finance Portal with supplier details, cost, and business justification.
2. Manager approves up to £5,000; Director approves up to £25,000; CFO approval required above £25,000.
3. The Finance team issues the PO number to the supplier.
4. Invoices must reference the PO number to be processed.

## Invoice Processing

Supplier invoices should be sent to invoices@acmecorp.com. Payment terms are 30 days from invoice date unless otherwise agreed. Invoices without a valid PO number or that do not match the PO amount will be placed on hold and the supplier notified.

## Budget Management

Department budgets are set annually in October/November as part of the planning cycle. Budget holders receive monthly reports by the 10th of each month. Variances greater than 10% or £5,000 (whichever is lower) must be explained in writing to the Finance team.

Budget transfers between line items require Finance Director approval. Transfers between departments require CFO approval.
