# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## Introduction

Welcome to Acme Corp. This handbook outlines our company policies, culture, and expectations. It applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees. Please read it carefully and refer back to it whenever you have questions about workplace policies.

## Working Hours and Flexibility

Standard working hours are 9:00 am to 5:30 pm, Monday through Friday. We operate a hybrid-first model: employees are expected to be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays, with full flexibility on the remaining days. Departments may agree to different arrangements with managerial sign-off.

Flexible start times are available between 7:30 am and 10:00 am, provided the employee completes their contracted hours and is reachable during core hours of 10:00 am–4:00 pm.

## Annual Leave

All employees receive 25 days of annual leave per year, in addition to public holidays. Leave must be requested through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance for absences of three or more consecutive days. For longer absences of two weeks or more, one month's notice is required.

Unused leave of up to 5 days may be carried over to the following year and must be used by the end of March. Employees leaving the company will be paid for any accrued, unused leave.

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. For absences of more than three consecutive days, a medical certificate is required. Sick leave does not accumulate year to year.

## Code of Conduct

We expect all employees to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism at all times. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated and will result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

All employees are expected to report any suspected ethical violations to the People & Culture team or via the anonymous ethics hotline.

## Remote Work Policy

Remote work is permitted subject to role requirements. Employees working remotely must maintain a stable internet connection, keep camera on during scheduled meetings unless exceptional circumstances apply, and be reachable via Slack during core hours.

Employees using company-issued equipment at home must follow the IT Security Policy with respect to VPN usage and data handling.

## Performance Reviews

Formal performance reviews are conducted twice per year, in June and December. Managers provide written feedback and employees complete a self-assessment. Performance ratings are linked to the annual bonus cycle, which is paid in February.

## Benefits

- Private health insurance (employee and immediate family)
- Pension contribution of 5% employer match, up to 5% employee contribution
- Monthly wellness budget of £50 (reimbursable against gym membership, fitness apps, or mental health services)
- Annual learning & development budget of £1,000 per employee
- Cycle to Work scheme
- Enhanced parental leave: 16 weeks full pay for primary caregivers, 4 weeks for secondary caregivers
