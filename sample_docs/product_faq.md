# Acme Platform – Product FAQ

## What does the Acme Platform do?

The Acme Platform is a cloud-based operations management suite that helps mid-sized businesses track projects, manage resources, and generate real-time performance reports. It connects to your existing tools via REST API and pre-built integrations for Slack, Jira, Salesforce, and Google Workspace.

## What plans are available?

| Plan | Monthly Price | Users | Storage | Support |
|------|--------------|-------|---------|---------|
| Starter | £99 | Up to 10 | 50 GB | Email (48h SLA) |
| Growth | £299 | Up to 50 | 500 GB | Email + chat (8h SLA) |

Annual billing offers a 20% discount across all plans.

## Is there a free trial?

Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose to subscribe or your workspace is archived (and can be restored within 30 days if you subscribe later).

## How is data stored and secured?

All data is stored in ISO 27001-certified data centres in the EU (Frankfurt primary, Dublin failover). Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. We are GDPR-compliant and can sign a Data Processing Agreement on request.

## Can I import data from other platforms?

Yes. The platform supports CSV import for projects, tasks, and contacts. We also offer a full REST API for programmatic data migration. For Enterprise customers, our onboarding team provides assisted migration at no additional cost.

## What integrations are available?

Currently supported integrations:
- **Communication:** Slack, Microsoft Teams
- **Issue tracking:** Jira, Linear, Asana
- **CRM:** Salesforce, HubSpot
- **Calendar:** Google Calendar, Outlook
- **Storage:** Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive
- **SSO:** Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace

Custom webhook and Zapier integration is available on Growth and Enterprise plans.

## What is the uptime SLA?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for Growth and Enterprise plans, measured monthly excluding scheduled maintenance. Starter plan customers receive best-effort uptime. Historical uptime is published at status.acmecorp.com.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from the billing settings page. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; you will not be charged again. Data is retained for 90 days post-cancellation before permanent deletion.

## How do I contact support?

- Email: support@acmecorp.com
- In-app chat: available on Growth and Enterprise plans
- Phone: Enterprise customers only, via your dedicated CSM

Support hours are Monday–Friday, 8:00 am–6:00 pm GMT.
